# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- College students booking university dormitory rooms.
- College administrators managing student room allocations, approvals, and support tickets.

## Product Purpose
The Digital Occupancy and Room Management System (D.O.R.M.S.) enables students to book rooms, check availability, and file support tickets, while administrators can track student eligibility and manage dormitory rooms online, replacing paper-based processes.

## Brand Personality
Simple, reliable, and clean. Emphasizes visual trust, ease of navigation, and clear structure.

## Anti-references
Legacy, clunky academic portals (dense tables, tiny input fields, lack of visual hierarchy, and nested boxes).

## Design Principles
- **Efficiency First**: Dashboards should present high-level metrics and actions clearly without visual noise.
- **Visual Rhythm**: Maintain generous, modern padding and consistent typography to keep administrative layouts readable.
- **Instant Affordances**: Buttons, statuses, and navigation elements must be clearly visible and distinguishable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for standard text, 3:1 for large/bold text).
- Responsive adaptation to support varied viewports (mobile/tablet/desktop).
